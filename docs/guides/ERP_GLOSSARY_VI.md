# ERP Glossary (Vietnamese Standard)

Muc tieu: thong nhat cach goi giua Accounting, Ecommerce, MRP de nguoi van hanh nho de hieu.

## Core Terms

- Dashboard: Tong quan
- Accounts: He thong tai khoan
- Journal Entry: But toan
- Fiscal Period: Ky ke toan
- Posting: Ghi so
- Receivable: Cong no phai thu
- Payable: Cong no phai tra

## Sales & Ecommerce

- Order: Don hang
- Order Number: So don hang
- Storefront: Cua hang truc tuyen
- Product: San pham
- Variant: Bien the
- Cart: Gio hang
- Payment: Thanh toan
- Refund: Hoan tien
- Shipment: Giao van
- Tracking Number: Ma van don

## Manufacturing (MRP)

- BOM: Dinh muc nguyen vat lieu
- Work Order: Lenh san xuat
- Routing: Tuyen cong doan
- Work Center: Tram san xuat
- WIP: Chi phi SXKD do dang
- Finished Goods: Thanh pham
- Scrap: Phe pham
- Rework: Tai che
- Lead Time: Thoi gian dan

## Suggested Status Labels

- DRAFT: Nhap
- PENDING: Cho xu ly / Cho duyet
- APPROVED: Da duyet
- POSTED: Da ghi so
- COMPLETED: Hoan tat
- CANCELLED: Da huy
- REFUNDED: Da hoan tien

## Rule of Thumb

1. Uu tien thuat ngu ngan gon, quen thuoc voi ke toan/van hanh VN.
2. Mot term tieng Anh chi map vao mot term tieng Viet duy nhat.
3. Neu co nguy co nham lan, hien thi dang `Viet (English)` o lan dau.
